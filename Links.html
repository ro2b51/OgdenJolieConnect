Images and Links

We are going to discuss how to put images and links on a page. We will discuss several types of links. Also, how to make clickable images.

External LInks

There are several types of links. There are internal, external, and locaal. External links go outside the server, meaning to another website.

Local Links

Local links are to another page in your same site. It is usually the navigation to direct you to another webpage. The navigation links are usually at the top of the page or down the left side.

Images

Use the img tag for an image. The src attribute allows you to put in the image file. The alt is for accessibility.
